Junjue Wang

State Key Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing

Wuhan University

I am a graduate student of photogrammetry and remote sensing at State Key Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing (LIESMARS), Wuhan University. I have received the B.S. degree from the School of Geography and Information Engineering, China University of Geosciences, Wuhan, China, in 2019. I'm now a member of RSIDEA group, advised by Prof. Yanfei Zhong and Liangpei Zhang.

My major research interests are high-resolution remote sensing imagery semantic segmentation and computer vision.
